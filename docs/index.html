Simple to Morph

Call a single function and plug that your animation library of choice. It isn't more complicated than that.

Backward Compatible

Use it on your least favorite platforms and see great results! It works great on modern browsers, too.

Super Lightweight

At about 6k minified and 3k gzipped, it is one of the smallest fully featured path morphing libraries available.
